What happens next?

The online deed you've viewed is not the legal deed.

  • You should sign and return your paper mortgage deed following the instructions you have received from your conveyancer
  • Land Registry will then update your title register to include the new mortgage

The time this takes will vary depending on the complexity of the transaction.
