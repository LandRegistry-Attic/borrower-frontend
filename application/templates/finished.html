Thank you for signing your
mortgage deed

What happens next?

  • If you are borrowing money with other people, they will need to sign the mortgage deed if they haven't already done this
  • Once all borrowers have signed the deed, your conveyancer will be notified so that they can continue to do the legal work needed for your mortgage
  • Land Registry will then update your title register to include the new mortgage

The time this takes will vary depending on the complexity of the transaction.
