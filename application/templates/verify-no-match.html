Sorry, you can't sign your mortgage deed online

Your identity has been verified but we have been unable to match your details with a mortgage deed held by Land Registry.

What do I do next?

  • You will need to sign a paper mortgage deed
  • You will need to tell your conveyancer Land Registry was unable to match your details to a digital mortgage deed
  • If other people are borrowing money with you, they will also need to sign a paper mortgage deed. They won’t need to use this online service

Can I still use Verify?

You do not need to re-register with Verify.

Even though you haven’t been able to use this online service, you will still be able to access other government services with your GOV.UK Verify account.
