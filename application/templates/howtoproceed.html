Sign your mortgage deed

Now that you’ve confirmed your identity you can sign your mortgage deed. You should:

  1. 1

    Agree to the Land Registry's terms and conditions for this service


  2. 2

    Read your mortgage deed carefully

    You’ll need to check the details of your mortgage deed and read through the legal clauses. If you spot any mistakes you should let the conveyancer know.

  3. 3

    Confirm your mortgage deed is correct

    If you are happy there are no mistakes, then you will need to request an authentication code to be sent to your mobile phone in order to sign the deed.

  4. 4

    Sign your mortgage deed

    Enter your authentication code to sign the deed. Doing so means that you don't need to sign a paper document, or get your signature witnessed.
